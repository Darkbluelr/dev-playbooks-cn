# /devbooks:challenger

Triggers Skill: `devbooks-proposal-challenger`

## Purpose

Challenge proposal.md and identify gaps, pointing out risks, omissions, inconsistencies with conclusions. Discover missing acceptance criteria and uncovered scenarios.

## Typical Use Cases

- Proposal completed and needs critical review
- Need to identify potential risks and omissions
- Execute Challenger role in proposal debate workflow

Please invoke `devbooks-proposal-challenger` to execute the corresponding task.
